About Me

Hi! I'm Sanaa Kapur and I am an 11th grade student aspiring to be a computer science student. I first started coding when I was 10 as part of a First Lego League robotics team. I loved the thrill of creating something new and wanted to learned more. Since then, I have explored several programming languages like Java, HTML/CSS, EV3, C++ and Python. I especially enjoy programming robots as it helps me see the applications of CS to the world at large.

When I am not coding, I enjoy practicing Kung Fu, reading, and spending time with my younger brother.
